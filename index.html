03 — Our Process

From harvest to pour

01

Direct Sourcing

We visit farms personally, build long-term relationships, and pay 40–60% above commodity price. Every bag traces back to a named farmer.

02

Green Grading

Every batch is cupped blind before purchase. We only buy specialty grade — 84+ points on the SCA scale, with zero primary defects.

03

Small Batch Roasting

Roasted in 12kg batches on a drum roaster. Each origin has a bespoke profile, developed over dozens of iterations until we hear the right crack.

04

Precise Brewing

93°C, 9 bar, 28 seconds. Or filter at 94°C for 3 minutes. We calibrate every method to express the bean's character, not mask it.

04 — Roast Science

The roast spectrum

Light Roast

Roasted to first crack only. The bean retains its origin character — floral, acidic, complex. Best for filter methods where clarity shines.
